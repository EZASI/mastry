MASTRY is not yet available for purchase. Prices (inclusive of Japanese consumption tax) will be clearly displayed on each product page at the time of sales launch. To be notified at launch, please join our founding-member list via the sign-up form on our website.

Additional Costs

The following costs may apply in addition to the product price and will be disclosed in full at launch:

Payment Method

Accepted payment methods will be confirmed and displayed at launch. We expect to support major credit cards and other common online payment options.

Payment Timing

Payment timing will be confirmed at launch and displayed clearly before the customer completes checkout. For card payments, the charge is generally processed at the time of order confirmation.

Delivery Timing

Estimated delivery lead times will be published at launch. MASTRY ships from Japan; international delivery timeframes will be shown per destination country at checkout. Domestic (Japan) delivery is expected within a few business days after order confirmation.

Returns and Exchanges

Full return and exchange terms (including the statutory right to cancel under relevant consumer protection law) will be published at the time of sales launch. In principle, we accept returns in the following circumstances:

  • The product is defective, damaged in transit, or materially different from what was ordered.
  • An incorrect item was delivered.

Customer-initiated returns for reasons of personal preference or ordering error may be subject to conditions and restocking or return-shipping costs. We will finalize and publish all terms before accepting any orders.

For any pre-launch enquiries about future terms, please contact us at hello@mastry.jp.
