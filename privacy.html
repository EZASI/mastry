2. Personal Information We Collect

We collect personal information only to the extent necessary for the purposes described below. The information we may collect includes:

  • Founding-member email signup: email address, and optionally your name.
  • Contact / inquiry form: name, email address, inquiry type, and the content of your message.
  • Access logs: browser type, IP address, pages visited, and date/time of access, collected automatically by our web server and analytics tools.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: see Section 8 below.

We do not collect payment card numbers, government-issued ID numbers, or other sensitive categories of personal information on this website.

3. Purposes of Use

We use the personal information we collect for the following specific purposes:

  • To send founding-member updates, product launch announcements, and exclusive pre-order information to subscribers who opt in.
  • To respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • To improve the content, usability, and performance of mastry.jp.
  • To detect and prevent unauthorised access, fraud, or abuse of our services.
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

We will not use your personal information beyond the stated purposes without your prior consent, except as permitted by applicable law.

4. Provision to Third Parties

MASTRY does not sell, rent, or otherwise provide your personal information to third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • You have given your explicit prior consent.
  • Provision is required by law or a valid order from a governmental authority.
  • It is necessary to protect a person's life, health, or property and obtaining consent is impracticable.
  • It is necessary for improving public health or promoting the sound growth of children and obtaining consent is impracticable.
  • A national agency, local government body, or their contractor requires the information to perform public duties prescribed by law, and obtaining your consent would impede that performance.

5. Outsourcing (Entrustment)

We may engage third-party service providers (such as email delivery platforms, web hosting providers, and analytics services) and share personal information with them to the extent necessary to perform those services on our behalf. We select providers carefully and require, by contract, that they handle personal information with equivalent security standards to our own and do not use it for any purpose other than the services contracted.

6. Safety Management Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal information against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, falsification, and leakage. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data in transit via TLS/HTTPS.
  • Access controls that restrict data to personnel with a legitimate need to process it.
  • Regular review of security practices and vendor assessments.

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to result in harm to data subjects, we will take prompt remedial action and make the required notifications under APPI.

7. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion Requests

Under the APPI, you have the right to request:

  • Notification of purpose of use — to be informed of the specific purposes for which we hold your personal information.
  • Disclosure — to receive a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction / addition / deletion — if any information is factually incorrect or no longer needed.
  • Suspension of use or erasure — where the information is being processed in violation of APPI.
  • Suspension of third-party provision — where third-party provision violates APPI.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@mastry.jp with sufficient information to verify your identity. We will respond within a reasonable period and, in any case, within the timeframe required by law. Please note that in certain circumstances defined by APPI we may be unable to fulfil a request, in which case we will explain the reason.

8. Cookies and Analytics

mastry.jp uses cookies and similar browser-based technologies to operate the site and understand how visitors use it. We may use Google Analytics or equivalent tools to collect aggregated, anonymised data (such as pages visited, session duration, and referral source). This data is processed in accordance with the analytics provider's own privacy policy and does not, by itself, identify you personally.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Doing so may limit some functionality of the site. You may also opt out of Google Analytics data collection by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use cookies to serve targeted advertising on mastry.jp.
